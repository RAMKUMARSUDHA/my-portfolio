About Me

Who Am I

I'm Ramkumar S, a passionate developer

I'm a web developer and AI enthusiast passionate about building apps that solve real problems. Currently in my pre-final year of B.Tech in AI & Data Science at Sri Eshwar College of Engineering, Coimbatore.

My flagship project is Tiffin Trail — a GPS-based street food vendor locator with three user roles, JWT authentication, WebSocket real-time updates, and Leaflet.js mapping. Earlier I shipped FarmGuard, a 20+ file farm compliance monitoring system with live Chart.js dashboards.

I interned as an AI engineer at CODTECH IT Solutions, building an AI chatbot and a blockchain health records system. My goal: an entry-level AI/ML engineering role.
